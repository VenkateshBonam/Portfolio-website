About Me

Hi, I'm Venkatesh Bonam — a UI/UX & Graphic Designer based in Hyderabad, India. I specialize in crafting clean, intentional interfaces that focus on clarity and usability. With 2 years of experience, I've worked across AI, e-commerce, fitness, and automation projects.

My approach combines Swiss design principles with modern user experience methodologies to create designs that are not only beautiful but also highly functional and accessible.
